Recommended Calculators

  • 1. Stock Return Calculator – computes % return: (Sell-Price + Dividends − Buy-Price) / Buy-Price.
  • 2. Future Value of Shares Calculator – given current price, expected growth rate & time horizon, what will it be.
  • 3. Average Buy Price Calculator – for multiple lots: total cost ÷ total shares. (e.g., averaging down)
  • 6. Position Size Calculator – given risk per trade, stop-loss distance, what size (number of shares) to buy.
  • 11. PE Ratio & Implied Value Calculator – given earnings and expected PE, what should price be.
  • 4. Holding Period Return Calculator – factoring in time, capital gain + dividends over a holding period.
  • 5. Reverse Return (Required Return) Calculator – given target price/time, what % return you need.
  • 7. Portfolio Weight Calculator – compute how much of a portfolio a given stock is (value ÷ total portfolio)
  • 8. Breakeven Price Calculator – factoring costs (brokerage, taxes) what price you must reach.
  • 9. Margin / Leverage Calculator – for margin trading: required capital, maximum allowable shares, etc.
  • 10. Stop-Loss / Take-Profit Calculator – given entry price and % stop/take, compute target levels.
  • 12. Dividend Yield & Future Dividend Calculator – yield now, expected growth, future dividend.
  • 13. Earnings Growth Impact Calculator – given earnings growth rate, compute future EPS and price.
  • 14. Dilution Impact Calculator – how issuance of new shares affects existing share value.
  • 15. Intrinsic Value Calculator (Discounted Cash Flow simplified) – project cash flows, discount rate, get value.
  • 16. Beta & Volatility Calculator – compute stock’s beta vs market and what that implies for risk.
  • 17. Value‐at‐Risk (VaR) Calculator for Single Stock – estimate worst‐case loss over a given time & probability.
  • 18. Sensitivity Calculator – how changes in growth rate / discount rate / margin affect valuation.
  • 19. Correlation / Covariance Calculator – assess how this stock moves with another stock or index.
  • 20. Scenario/Stress Test Calculator – run best-case, base-case, worst-case for stock price or portfolio.
  • 21. Trailing Stop Recalculation Calculator – for dynamic stop loss based on movement of stock price.
  • 22. Stock Splits & Bonus Impact Calculator – effect of stock splits, bonus shares on cost and quantity.
  • 23. Tax/After‐Tax Return Calculator – given capital gain, dividends, compute after‐tax return.
  • 24. Dividend Reinvestment Calculator (DRIP) – if you reinvest dividends, how much would you have after time.
  • 25. Time to Target Price Calculator – given current growth/return rate, estimate time to reach a target price.
